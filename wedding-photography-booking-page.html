booking o&c

 

First of all download our wedding brochure to find out exactly who we are and what you get. All the information you need is there but incase we’ve missed anything off send us an email at hello@oacphotography.com and we’ll get back to you with an answer.

 

If then you are interested in booking us as your wedding photographers follow the steps below:

  • Email hello@oacphotography.com and check that your date is available
  • Arrange your No obligation consultation. This is a chance to meet up and chat through your plans.
  • If you like what you see sign our contract
  • Pay your £450 deposit to secure your booking
  • Arrange our first meeting and venue visit.
  • Breathe out, sit back. Relax, make a cuppa and enjoy ticking off the “wedding photographer” box on your plans

 

Once we have received your deposit your date is held exclusively for you.

 

The remaining balance must be paid in full by four weeks before your wedding.

 

If you are not sure then we offer all enquiries a free no obligation consultation to give you a change to meet us and have a look at the kind of things we provide.  If you think that sounds like you please get in touch and we’ll organise a date.
